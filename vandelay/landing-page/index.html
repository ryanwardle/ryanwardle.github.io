Our Story

In 2013 architect Arthur Van Delay was drinking his morning cup of coffee. The coffee was expensive but the taste left much to be desired. He began to wonder about where the coffee came from and if it was possible to get better quality coffee, at a more reasonable price. Arthur started to ask himself questions. Was the price of the coffee justified? Was the price worth it? Was it possible to get a better cup of coffee for a better price? No, no, and yes! Arthur soon quit his job as an architect to start Van Delay coffee. The reason we are able to sell coffee to consumers at such a low price is because we buy our product directly from some of the best coffee farmers in the entire world, effectively making us a wholesaler. Instead of buying expensive coffee from a store who has greatly marked up the price, you are able to buy small batches of coffee directly from us at wholesale prices.

Pricing

Seasonal

Each season Van Delay Coffee provides a seasonal blend of coffee. For the summer we have El Chino, farmed by the Maidana family just outside Buenos Aires, Argentina. El Chino is a medium roast coffee with hints of chocolate and raspberry.

$12.99 / lb

Coffee of the Month

Similar to our seasonal coffee, we also provide a coffee of the month. This allow us to provide fresh, new flavors to our customers on a frequent basis. This month we have Classic Kona. Classic Kona was grown in, you guessed it, Kona, Hawaii. It is a medium roast coffee with hints of walnut and cherry.

$14.99 / lb

Wake Up

Our Wake Up blend is always a bold, dark roast coffee to help energize you for your day. Our Wake Up blends change every 2-3 months, we currently have Canelo, farmed in Oaxaca, Mexico. Canelo's taste profile includes hints of cinnamon, and toasted almonds.

$13.49 / lb
